Taylor Gariepy is a 19 year old environmental science student at The University of Ottawa. She has been lashing for 2 years and has expanded her business to reach all corners of the Ottawa area. She earned her Lash techinican certfication from Viva Lashes.

Types of Sets

Classic

Classic lash sets are done by applying an individual lash to an individual lash. Classic sets are associated with giving a more natural and light look.

Hybrid

Hybrid lash sets are done by applying a combination of single lashes and clusters of lashes. Hybrid sets are perfect to give you a fuller lash line while staying on the natural side.

Volume

Volume lash sets are created by applying clusters of lashes to an individual lash. Volume sets give fullness, drama, and are perfect for going out.

Lash Upkeep

Tending to your lashes is key in longer lasting lusciousness. Brushing and cleaning your lashes everyday when you wake up and before you go to bed is vital. Using a spooly (provided at each session) and Micellar water, carefully brush through your lashes. Oil based cleansers will eat away at the glue, so make sure you only used water based tear-free cleansers. (ask the technician by leaving a question for more information).
